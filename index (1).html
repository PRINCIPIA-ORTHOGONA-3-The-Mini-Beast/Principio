Assignments & Lessons

Language learning grounded in the Principia Orthogona series

Each lesson connects real academic research, history, and science to English language learning. Read the paper. Watch the video. Discuss the ideas. Write your response.

About this series. These lessons are designed for the Hour House Adult ESL programme at 229 Ballantine Pkwy, Newark, NJ 07104. Levels range from B1 to C1. Each lesson is self-contained but builds on a shared framework — the TO/TOGT operator series (Principia Orthogona). Lessons 06–07 introduce the WorldQuant Brain platform, where students can compete in the International Quant Championship and contribute to the USA aggregate score.
